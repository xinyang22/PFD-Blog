Week 1 blog

I am a student at Ngee Ann Polytechnic. For my portfolio development project, I am developing a new way to track employee attendance using bluetooth/RFID gantries. I am working with a team of other students on this project. The system is still in the early stages of development, but we have already identified a list of equipment and software we will need. I am excited about the potential of this system to improve efficiency and accuracy in employee attendance tracking. I believe that the system could be used in a variety of industries, including healthcare, manufacturing, and retail. I will keep you updated on the progress of this project.

Week 3 blog

My team have successfully made the Arduino tag and reader work.Our system uses Bluetooth/RFID gantries to track employee attendance. When an employee walks through a gantry, their tag is scanned and their attendance is recorded in our database. This system is much more efficient and accurate than traditional methods of attendance tracking, such as sign-in sheets. My group has also finalise what each member is going to do so that we will be more efficient.
